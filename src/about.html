Due to social distancing, thousands of us have found ourselves needing to go out only when strictly necessary, and regardless of our daily tasks and obligations now from home, we all need a space for recreation, a time when we can relax and relive the emotions that our loved and hated "Game of Thrones" has brought us.

Game of Thrones is the most popular dramatic and fantasy TV series of the last few years. It is recognized by millions of people around the world.

In its more than 8 years of with a total of 8 seasons and 73 chapters it continues to attract new followers and offers a different experience to those who follow the plot from the beginning.
From the comparison with the books, the admiration or repulsion for one of its characters, the aspects and themes of the series, to its unexpected end. Any element can be a reason to love or hate this series. But what is Game of Thrones about? What are its characters? What are the Seven Kingdoms? What are its themes? Let's get to know the details of this successful series.

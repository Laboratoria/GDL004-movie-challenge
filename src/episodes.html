"Winter Is Coming"


It has been 15 years since the last war ravaged the former monarchy of the Targaryen, whose king was the Mad King. Since then, it is King Baratheon who is in charge of the monarchical dynasty together with his chief advisor who holds the title 'Hand of the King', Ned Stark. Likewise, the rest of the Houses will be known, which will echo a news that will change the future of all of them.
